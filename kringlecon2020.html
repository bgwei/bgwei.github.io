Kringlecon 2020

Three French Hens

Kringlecon is an annual hacking challenge that takes place around the holidays each year. This year marks the third Kringlecon, hence the subtitle. Kringlecon is formatted as a holiday-themed browser game, and this year the player finds themself taking the New Jersey Turnpike to Kringle Castle at the North Pole. Upon arrival, the player is tasked with helping Santa's elves with a wide range of problems ranging from using RegExes to fix Santa's present sorter to detecting and reversing malware on Santa's system. The challenge is a fun opportunity to learn and can be found here so you can hop right in and save Christmas!
